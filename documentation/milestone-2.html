PWP Milestone 2

Content Strategy

The website will be a single-page design with the following sections:

Hero Section

Features a stunning crystal/jewelry hero image with overlay text introducing the brand and a clear value proposition. Includes a prominent call-to-action button.

Crystal Education

Interactive grid showcasing different crystals, their properties, and uses. Each crystal card will expand to show detailed information and related jewelry pieces.

Jewelry Portfolio

Responsive gallery featuring handcrafted pieces with filtering options by crystal type and jewelry category. Each piece will have detailed descriptions and crystal information.

Custom Orders

Information about the custom jewelry process, including a simple form for inquiries and timeline expectations.

Contact

Email signup form for crystal education content and new piece announcements, plus a contact form for direct inquiries.
